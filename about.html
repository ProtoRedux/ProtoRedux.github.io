About The Artist



Gloomblight is a British Illustrator currently residing in the South West of England. Her work is inspired by a number of historical art movements such as the Renaissance, Baroque, Rococo and Romanticism.
She developed her style from years of watching old Disney films as a child and discovering Anime and Manga as a teenager.
Gloomblight is currenty a second year university student and has plans to undertake a career in freelance illustration after graduation. Working predominantly around illustrating literature.
Her work is dark and eerie, but not with the intent to unsettle, but to demonstrate beauty through an unlikely aesthetic.
When she isn't working, Gloomblight enjoys reading old novels and playing video games.
